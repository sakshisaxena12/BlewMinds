Alcoholic Leadership

BANGALORE, India
January 2018





Being a change-maker is not easy. It can drain you completely if you don’t take care of yourself. It is a boon being able to touch so many people’s lives and turning tables for good. But it can also be very taxing on one’s own self. There is a way in which each drink tastes its best, each spice makes the dish more palatable, each colour stands out and blends in. A good leader understands their strength and power, the delicate mix of their emotional balance. What emotion are you serving? And to whom? Alcoholic Leadership is BlewMinds’ flagship Leadership product which aims to explore the insightful & deeply metaphorical concept of Leadership with Alcohol. It aims to remove the stigma attached to alcohol in a conservative set up & help individuals understand that right/wrong & good/bad are all wolves that delve within us & they are situation dependent. BlewMinds partnered with the very intelligent Dr Richard Claydon, to drive home the 10 powerful Commandments of Alcoholic Leadership in a MasterClass in Bangalore, India in Jan 2018. It is based on very fascinating tenets including exploring the Lust of Power Vs Lust of Alcohol, Are you holding your drink or is your drink holding you? This very effective framework is deployed by BlewMinds for Executive Impact & Executive Presence Leadership Group Coaching across organizations. 19 Jan 2018 was filled with deep discussions around Alcoholic Leadership, Organizational Toxicity & Misbehaviour in organizations across the globe today. The Jan 2018 event MasterClass ended with Q&A, Drinks & a Networking Dinner with about 70+ Leaders in the room interacting & participating in all zeal.
