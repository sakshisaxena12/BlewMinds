DELHI NCR, India
November 2017

Event By: By John Mattone






BlewMinds launched the very first Intelligent Leadership Coaching Program in Delhi NCR, India in Nov 2017 very successfully. Participants had an incredibly enlightening experience given the depth of conversations, global frameworks deployed & scientific tools used. It had World’s #2 Coach, John Mattonedeeply invested in aiding these Coachees to move towards a stronger inner core. BlewMinds & John together coached, trained & enabled participants move towards sustainable growth & progress. This deep framework of Intelligent Leadership Coaching certification aims to build Future-Ready coaches who can challenge status quo, change the world & its corporate culture by becoming the best version of Leaders they can become! The Coaching Certification Program wasalso coupled with a CXO MasterClass being conducted for a large niche audience of 75+ Leaders of Delhi NCR, India. John shared his story of being Steve Jobs’ Coach, his learning through Coaching & how he managed to become what he is today after a series of so-called failures. The session concluded with fascinating Questions & Answers, Drinks & Networking Dinner. BlewMinds Founder & Storyteller Sandeep Kochhar also addressed the audience with his signature style of incredibly engrossing StoryTelling. BlewMinds Co-Founder Vishwapriya Kochhar also addressed the crowd with an insightful Vote of Thanks & she ran through her entire life like a movie flashing before our eyes while she did this.
